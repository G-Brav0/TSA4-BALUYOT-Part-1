Mocha Frappe


A shot of espresso is combined with a chocolate powder or syrup, followed by milk or cream.

Iced Matcha


Has a flavor profile with mellow vegetal grassy notes, natural sweet nuttiness, a touch of bitterness with a pleasant savory ending.

Brewed Coffee


Made by pouring hot water over ground coffee beans and allowing the mixture to steep for several minutes, allowing the water to extract the flavour and aroma of the coffee beans.

Caramel Machiatto


It is made with vanilla syrup, steamed milk, espresso, and caramel sauce. The espresso shot is poured on top of the milk leaving a dark mark on top of the milk foam.
